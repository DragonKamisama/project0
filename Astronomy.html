Aquarius Zodiac Constellation This constellation takes the form of a mermaid or a maiden with a water pitcher.
Pisces Zodiac Constellation The second zodiac symbol draws the image of two fish tied together with a string.
Aries Zodiac Constellation One of the twelve zodiac constellations. It takes the form of a ram.
Taurus Zodiac Constellation This is the bull and is very prominent in the northern hemisphere for most of the year.
Gemini Zodiac Constellation This constellation takes the form of the twins, Pollux and Castor. The heads of the twins are represented by the stars, appropriately named, Pollux and Castor.
Cancer Zodiac Constellation This is the crab. It is a fairly simple structure and easy to spot once Leo and Gemini are located.
Leo Zodiac Constellation This is the legendary Nemean Lion that was slain by Hercules. It can be spotted as a sitting lion. The mane of the lion is recognised through an inverted question mark. The brightest star in the constellation is called Regulus or Alpha Leonis.
Virgo Zodiac Constellation This constellation is in the form of an innocent maiden.
Libra Zodiac Constellation The scales is the symbol of Libra. Like Cancer it has a very simple structure and represents balance.
Scorpius Zodiac Constellation This is the scorpion constellation. The tail of the scorpion is also rcognised as the hook weapon of the demigod Maui.
Sagittarius Zodiac Constellation This constellation is in the form of a centaur, a half man - half horse creature. It is seen shooting arrows into the night sky and is called The Archer.
Capricorn Zodiac Constellation One of the twelve zodiac constellations. It takes the form of a mountain sea-goat.
