Astronomy
I love Astronomy! I especially like stargazing and using constellations to track directions. I would like to get into research as a career in the future. I am studying to get my bachelors degree in Astrophysics. In the future, I aim to conduct research in the field of cosmology. I would also like to work with black-holes and its various forms such as quasars and blazars. To enjoy a list of constellations and some funky images click here!
Reading
